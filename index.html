About Us

At BobaBae we're passionate about bringing you the best bubble tea in the UAE. We use only the best ingredients and authentic recipes to create our delicious and refreshing drinks.

We offer a wide variety of flavours and toppings to choose from, so you're sure to find something to your taste. Whether you're a fan of classic bubble milk tea or you're looking for something more adventurous, we have somthing for everyone. We also offer a variety of vegan and gluten-free options.

We're committed to providing our customers with the best possible experience. That's why we use only the highest quality ingredients and our staff is always happy to help you create the perfect bubble tea.
